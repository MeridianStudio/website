Common questions

What to
expect.

Do I need to prepare anything?

Yes — we'll send a short prep guide when you book. You'll need your logo, any copy you want to use, and a few photos if applicable. The more ready you are, the smoother the day runs.

What if I need more than 5 pages?

If your project is bigger, it might be better suited to Meridian's full engagement. We'll tell you honestly after a quick conversation.

Which platform will my site be built on?

We'll recommend the best fit based on your needs — typically Framer, Webflow, or Squarespace. You'll be able to manage it yourself after handoff.

Can I make changes after launch?

Yes. You'll own and control the site. The 30-day support period covers minor tweaks — larger changes can be scoped separately.
